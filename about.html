Welcome to Epoch

Epoch is a machine learning club at Indian Institute of Information Technology, Sricity.

What are we?

Wanna get ahead of the local minima before everyone else? Think you can tune your hyper parameters just right? Want to add momentum to your machine learning journey?
Well then Epoch is the perfect place to be! Epoch is a club to bring Machine learning and Artificial Intelligence enthusiasts together.


We at Epoch conduct

  • Group discussions, Paper reviews
  • Workshops, Hands on sessions
  • Even introductory educational sessions for beginners!
