Frequently Asked Questions

Epoch is a student-run club at IIIT Sricity dedicated to machine learning (ML) and artificial intelligence (AI) enthusiasts. The club aims to foster a community where members can learn, share, and collaborate on ML and AI projects.
Any student at IIIT Sricity with an interest in machine learning and artificial intelligence can join Epoch. The club welcomes beginners as well as those with more advanced knowledge.
Members can stay updated through the club’s official communication channels, and also subscription to our newsletters through website, social media groups, and bulletin boards within the institute.
Epoch provides opportunities to work on real-world projects, often through collaborations with faculty, industry partnerships, and hackathons. These projects can help members gain practical experience.
Epoch provides access to various learning resources such as tutorials, datasets, research papers, and recommended reading materials. The club also facilitates peer learning and mentorship.
